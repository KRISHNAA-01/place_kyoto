Nidec Kyoto Tower

Kyoto Tower is an observation tower located in Kyoto, Japan. The steel tower is the tallest structure in Kyoto with its observation deck at 100 metres and its spire at 131 metres. The 800-ton tower stands atop a 9-story building, which houses a 3-star hotel and several stores.

Fushimi Inari Taisha

Fushimi Inari-taisha (伏見稲荷大社) is a renowned Shinto shrine located in the Fushimi-ku area of southern Kyoto, Japan. It serves as the head shrine (総本宮, sōhongū) of Inari Ōkami (稲荷大神), the god of rice, prosperity, and business.

Arashiyama Bamboo Grove

The Arashiyama Bamboo Grove (also known as Sagano Bamboo Forest) is a natural bamboo forest located in the Arashiyama district of Kyoto, Japan. It's renowned for its towering bamboo stalks that create a serene and ethereal atmosphere, and is considered one of Kyoto's most iconic sights.
